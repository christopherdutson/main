About Me

I developed a love for Book of Mormon Baseball on my mission. Granted, it was basically the only game we could play, but also a great one. It's also a fun way to learn the scriptures.

My dream for the last few years has been to program my own Book of Mormon game, and now we have the funding to start the project! It won't be easy, but the whole crew believes in us.
